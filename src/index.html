Salmon Croquettes

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Salmon
  • 1 sleeve of Saltines
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic
  • 2 teaspoons of onion powder
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a large bowl empty the contents of salmon. Pick out any bones and scales to be left with just the meat of the salmon
  2. Take your sleeve of saltines and crush it up real well. The more you crush it the better the croquettes will taste. Once they are crushed we'll add to the bowl and mix it in
  3. Add eggs and seasonings and mix until fully incorporated
  4. Shape into desired patty size and heat up a skillet on medium high heat
  5. Place 1 to 2 patties into the skillet and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side
  6. Once cooked transfer to a lined plate to cool and enjoy
